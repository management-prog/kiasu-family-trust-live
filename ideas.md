# Design Philosophy: The Gilded Heritage

## Design Movement
**Classical Editorial / High Renaissance**
A sophisticated, narrative-driven aesthetic that evokes the permanence and prestige of old-world institutions. It combines the authority of classical heraldry with the refined layout of high-end editorial print.

## Core Principles
1.  **Permanence & Authority:** Every visual element should feel weight, established, and timeless.
2.  **Narrative Richness:** The design frames content like a story or a legal decree, using borders, textures, and typography to elevate the text.
3.  **Understated Opulence:** Wealth is implied through material quality (textures, colors) rather than flashy effects.
4.  **Restraint:** Use of negative space and structured layouts to convey confidence and exclusivity.

## Color Philosophy
**"Deep Ocean & Sovereign Gold"**
-   **Primary Background:** Rich Navy (`#1a2744`) - Represents depth, stability, and the vastness of the ocean (nod to Puerto Rico location and global reach).
-   **Primary Accent:** Luminous Gold (`#b8860b`) - Used for gradients, borders, and key typography. Represents wealth, heritage, and the "gilded" nature of the trust.
-   **Secondary Background:** Cream/Off-White (for paper texture areas) or deep charcoal for contrast.
-   **Text:** Off-white on Navy, Dark Charcoal on Cream.

## Layout Paradigm
**"The Framed Manuscript"**
-   Avoid full-width fluid layouts for text.
-   Use "framed" content areas that mimic heavy cardstock or official documents.
-   Central axis alignment for headings (heraldic style), but balanced with asymmetrical editorial blocks for body copy.
-   Borders are essential: double lines, gold leaf edges, or corner flourishes.

## Signature Elements
1.  **The Eagle Seal:** A heraldic eagle (visual only, no text) used as a watermark, a stamp, or a central medallion.
2.  **Linen/Paper Texture:** Subtle background textures to give a tactile feel, moving away from digital flatness.
3.  **Gold Gradients:** Linear gradients on borders and buttons to simulate metallic reflection.

## Interaction Philosophy
**"Deliberate & Weighted"**
-   Hover effects should be slow and smooth (e.g., a border glowing gold, a card lifting slightly).
-   No rapid, bouncy animations.
-   Transitions should feel like turning a heavy page or opening a wax-sealed envelope.

## Animation
-   **Entrance:** Elements "fade up" slowly with a slight scale increase, as if coming into focus.
-   **Scroll:** Parallax effects on the paper textures or the eagle watermark.

## Typography System
-   **Headings:** **Cormorant Garamond** (Serif) - Elegant, high-contrast, old-style. Used for all titles and pull quotes.
-   **Body:** **IBM Plex Sans** (Sans-serif) - Clean, technical, and modern. Provides a grounded contrast to the ornate headings, suggesting competence and analytical rigor (fitting for a quant/investment focus).
